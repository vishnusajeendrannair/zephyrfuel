Technology

AEM Electrolysis.
Engineered for
Industry.

Our Anion Exchange Membrane electrolyzers combine the efficiency of PEM systems with the cost advantages of alkaline technology — purpose-built for continuous industrial hydrogen supply.

Each module uses four 125 kW cassettes operating in parallel, with 132 cells per cassette and 500 cm² active area per cell — delivering approximately 240 kg of H₂ per day per module.

Business Model

SPV Project Finance

Each deployment is capitalized through a Special Purpose Vehicle — ring-fencing project risk and enabling non-recourse debt financing.

01

Feasibility Study

Paid engagement ($5K–$75K+) validates site economics, power costs, and off-take volumes. Fee credited toward future contract.

→
02

SPV Formation

Project-level entity formed per site. 70% project debt / 30% equity. ZephyrFuel retains asset ownership throughout the term.

→
03

Deployment

Modular electrolyzer systems installed and commissioned. ~$3.27M per-site capex. Customer operations integration included.

→
04

Supply Agreement

Long-term hydrogen supply contract. Customer pays per kg delivered. ZephyrFuel operates and maintains the asset for the full term.
